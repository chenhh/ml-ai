# 最小平方解\(least square solution\)

## 簡介

* 矩陣$$A \in F^{M \times N} , A=\begin{bmatrix} A_{:1} & \dots & A_{:N}\end{bmatrix}= \begin{bmatrix} A_{1:} \\ \vdots \\ A_{M:} \end{bmatrix}, A^{\mathrm{H}}=  \begin{bmatrix} \overline{A_{1:}} & \dots & \overline{A_{M:1}}\end{bmatrix}$$
* 例如：$$A=\begin{bmatrix} a_{11} & a_{12} \\ a_{21} & a_{22} \\ a_{31} & a_{32}\end{bmatrix}$$，$$A^{\mathrm{H}}=\begin{bmatrix} \overline{a_{11}} & \overline{a_{21}} & \overline{a_{31}} \\ \overline{a_{12}} & \overline{a_{22}} & \overline{a_{32}} \end{bmatrix}$$
